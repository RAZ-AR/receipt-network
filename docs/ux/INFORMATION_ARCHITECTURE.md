# Information Architecture

## Primary navigation

Recommended MVP bottom navigation:

1. Home
2. Rewards
3. Scan
4. Lottery
5. Profile

The scan action is visually dominant.

## Home

- selected reward goal;
- points balance;
- active lottery tickets;
- next draw;
- opportunity of the day;
- recent receipt result;
- scan CTA.

## Rewards

- active goal;
- available rewards;
- reward categories;
- nearby or digital rewards;
- reward details;
- redemption history.

## Scan

- camera;
- gallery import;
- batch capture;
- upload queue;
- processing results;
- rescan and correction paths.

## Lottery

- active tickets;
- current draw;
- prizes;
- rules;
- draw history;
- wins and claim status.

## Profile

- personal details;
- receipt history;
- points ledger;
- reviews;
- notifications;
- privacy;
- support.

## Secondary destinations

- receipt details;
- review form;
- reward claim;
- notification center;
- support ticket;
- legal rules;
- partner business profile.
